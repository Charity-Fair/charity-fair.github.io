Charity Fair - Bread for the World

About:

Bread for the World is a Christian advocacy organization urging U.S. decision
makers to do all they can to pursue a world without hunger. Our mission is to
educate and equip people to advocate for policies and programs that can help end
hunger in the U.S. and around the world.

Food banks are also essential –- but we focus on advocacy. Why? Because federal
nutrition programs provide roughly 10 times as much food assistance as private
churches and charities combined. With the stroke of a pen, our government
leaders have the power to create policies and programs that impact millions of
people at once.

Our faith in Christ is the foundation for our mission and compels us to love our
neighbors near and far. We are led by the values of our faith to stand up for those
experiencing hunger all over.

“Open your mouth for those who cannot speak, and for the rights of those who are
